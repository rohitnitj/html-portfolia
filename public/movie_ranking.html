The Best Movies According to Rohit

My top 3 movies of all-time.

M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story

It is based on the life of former Test, ODI and T20I captain of the Indian national cricket team, Mahendra Singh Dhoni.

3 Idiots

It is a comedy-drama that follows the lives of three engineering students in India.

PK

The story revolves around an alien named PK (played by Aamir Khan), who lands on Earth and loses the remote to his spaceship.
